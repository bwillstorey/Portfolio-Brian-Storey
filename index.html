About Me

I love to learn.

As long as I can remember, I always enjoyed discovering new things and figuring out how they worked. A new discovery would hook me and I'd dive in to learn everything I could about it. Whether it be a musical instrument, a theory, or a computer program, I enjoy the process of breaking down each system to understand how they work together to make up the finished product. Even more exciting is discovering new ways to make improvements to those systems.

I have found a calling in software development. Not only is there endless learning, but the process of deconstructing and building is a natual fit and something I deeply enjoy. I currently am enrolled in a full-stack software development program at Vanderbilt University to build upon my skillset and become a more proficient programmer.

I have also become heavily involved in data anlaytics in my career, leveraging data to provide business insights to senior leaders. Analytics and development have become a powerful pairing that I am excited to continue to grow.
